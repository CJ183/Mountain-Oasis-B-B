About the town

Steamboat Springs is a small town in Colorado.
The main activity is walking through the shops of the town. Whether you're looking for a sweet treat, ski rentals, merchandise, or gifts for loved ones, this is the town for you.


Your next activity will be checking out the Mountain. Rent a pair of skis or a snowboard in the town and then hit the slopes. The mountain staff are extremely experienced. They even offer lessons for kids and adults. Whether your're a first timer or well-versed, this is the place for you.


















Lastly, decompress with the hot springs. There are both free hot springs and official spas with natural hotsprings inside. They vary in temperatures and are perfect for relaxing muscles after a day on the Mountain.
