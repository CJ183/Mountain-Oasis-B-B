History of Steamboat

Steamboat was originally founded by the Ute Native Americans for the medicianl hot springs.
The natural mineral springs sometimes make sounds that sound like a steamboat chugging, hence the town's name Steamboat Springs.
The town started out with three hotels, three stables, three banks, four stores, and two meat markets.
Once the railroad came to town, the commercial industry took off and the town became a booming business
