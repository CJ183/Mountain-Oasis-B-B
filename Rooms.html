Room Options

Our first room is on the first floor of the Bed and Breakfast.
Handicap accesible, it is the perfect way to explore the town without searching for accomodations.
This room also offers wifi and a T.V.









On the second floor, we have 3 single rooms with queen beds.
These rooms are perfect for couples looking for a weekend getaway.
Each room features a T.V and wifi access.
The rooms are cozy spaces that provide comfort after a long day of hiking, swimming, skiing, and exploring the town.









Lastly, on the second floor we have a suite.
This is two rooms connected by a Jack and Jill bathrooms.
On one side, is a single room with a queen size bed and on the other side is a double room with two twin beds.
The suite is perfect for families looking to get away for a bit.
