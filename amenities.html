Our bed and breakfast offers many amenities to make you feel at home while traveling.

First, we offer a sitting room with a T.V. Enjoy a movie night with fellow travelers or just get to know them.



















We also have handicap ramps to allow access to the first floor room.
Each room is equiped with a safe for valuables. We also offer houskeeping for the rooms. This can be requested during your stay, otherwise it happens before check-in and after check-out.
We also have a beautiful lake right outside. Sit by the campfire at night and enjoy the light waves or take a swim in the hot summer sun.


















Lastly, we have Brekafast each morning. There is a rotating menu each day. Click the link in the navigation bar above to view our breakfast menu
